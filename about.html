Home Emergency Cover

Cover your entire home with our most comprehensive Home Emergency assistance package today! Home Emergency Insurance through Protect You gives peace of mind that in the event of a domestic emergency, you have access to professionally qualified engineers capable of dealing with emergencies 24 hours a day! Take out our Complete Cover and protect your home against a wide range of emergencies. Protect yourself against the cost of repairing broken boilers, burst pipes, blocked drains, water pipe leaks, electrical wiring damage, pest infestations and damaged roofing. The policy is underwritten by UK General Insurance and includes the cost of the engineers call out fee, all parts and materials as well as labour to get the emergency sorted.
